    FAQ

    —

    What is Fearless?

    Fearless is a mobile wallet app for the Kusama network, with support on iOS and Android platforms. Fearless is focused on providing the best user experience, fast performance, and secure storage for your accounts.

    —

    When will it be available?

    The first release, which will include Account Management, Wallet, App Settings, and Extrinsic Support will be available in the middle of September 2020. Staking and Governance releases will come before the end of 2020.

    —

    Who is developing Fearless?

    Mobile apps are developed by Soramitsu, with support from the Kusama Treasury. Soramitsu is a Japanese technology company delivering blockchain-based solutions for enterprises, universities, and governments. From the creation of domestic and cross-border payment systems, to the development of a completely decentralized autonomous economy, Soramitsu's projects and use case studies represent the next-generation of fintech.

    —

    What is Kusama?

    Kusama is a scalable network of specialized blockchains built using Substrate and nearly the same codebase as Polkadot.

    —

    Does that mean Kusama is a testnet for Polkadot?

    No. This is a popular misconception. Kusama is not Polkadot's testnet, and you can find more information about it on the web-site: kusama.network
